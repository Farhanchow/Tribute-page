Celestial Being

A paramilitary organization founded by GN technology pioneer and scientist, Aeolia Schenberg

The private paramilitary organization Celestial Being, which pursues the complete eradication of armed conflict from Earth was planned and envisioned by its founder, Schenberg 200 years prior to it becoming fully functional. The main objective of Celestial Being's activities was to completely eliminate acts of war from this world by means of amred interventions. The organization established under the ideology of ending global conflict using a line of revolutionary mobile weapons known as 'Gundams'. These mobile weapons operated under GN drives, which were made with GN particles. These Gundam Nucleus (GN)particles are exotic particles created from years of experimentation of electromagentic waves and gravity. GN particles are prominently used as part of an electromagnetic radar-disruption stealth measure, a physical barrier, GN Vernier propellant, and a particle coating that enhances both the sharpness (aka. cutting ability) of physical blades and the durability of armor. Celestial Being utilized the whole GN technology to develop these movile weapons for the sole purpose of to rid ourselves of the scourge of war. Their philosophy was openly stated during their debut by the declaration to all humanity. Territory, religion, energy no matter what the reason or excuse if there is an evident act of war being carried out, Celestial Being would commence intervention with their force. Any country, organization or corporation that promotes war were a legitimate target for their intervention.

History

  • July 2307 A.D. - Celestial Beings declaration to the world and debut.
  • February 2308 - 72 hours battle with Advanced European Union, ending in termination of all European weapon development project.
  • August 2309 - United Peace Keeping Force established to eliminate Celestial Being.
  • March 2310 - Celestial Beings GN Technology was compromised by United Nation Forces.
  • December 2311 - Celestial Being develops a new technology called Trans-Am system using GN particles on their mobile weapons to counter stolen GN Tech by the United Nation Forces.
  • August 2312 - All Earth armed forces loses against Celestial Being. All Earth citizens united and allied with Celestial Being.
  • January 2338 - Celestial Being faces new threat from another planet. Makes first contact with an alien race.
  • June 2339 - Celestial Being unites all humanity with this alien race. Peace established through understanding each other. Celestial Being no longer needs to carry out any form of armed intervention on planet Earth or any other region in the universe. The organization will become active once again when a new threat appears to destroy this peace.
